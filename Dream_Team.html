About

Dream Team is a round-based board game about dreams. Players start in a dream flat that becomes increasingly plagued by nightmares. To defend their dream they must construct 3 Dream Pillars.Strategically using tiles, cards, abilities and communication combined with the exploration of a procedurally generated map form the core of the gameplay and is essential for success.

The game was created with a team of five and over a period of 6 weeks at the end of the second semester. The task was to develop a game within certain constraints, including Optimization for two Players, Family Friendly, Compact-Rules and Distinct World-theme. Nice to have features that were not implemented in the game due time constraints including, different layers, progression system, artworks for every card and more.
