About me

I am a student in the 3rd semester of Games and Immersive Media, and I love to express my ideas in a creative and playful way.

I am interested in game design, 2/3D Art, Animation and modeling, with a particular passion for Pixel-art, worldbuilding and how a world and its activities would function as a system.

In my free time, I create Pixel-art, sketches, and develop concepts for characters, objects and systems such as production lines, crafting mechanics, magic systems, cities or whatever comes to mind.
