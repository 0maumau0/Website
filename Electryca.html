About

Electryca is a horror escape game set in an old abandoned factory. The player must solve riddles in order to escape from the factory, while realizing that they may not be alone in the dark. Fear, exploration and riddles are the main focuses of the gameplay.

The game was created by a team of five over approximately 3 weeks at the beginning of the first semester. The goal was to dive straight into game development, challenge ourselves and learn the workflow of agile project development.
